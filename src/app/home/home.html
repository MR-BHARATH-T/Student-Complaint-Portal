Student Complaint Portal

A secure and user-friendly platform for students to submit, track, and manage their complaints efficiently. Powered by Angular 20 and Firebase, with beautiful UI using Bootstrap and smooth animations.

Easy Registration & Login

Register with validated fields for name, SRN, email, phone, department, and course. Secure authentication with Firebase.

Submit Complaints

Post complaints with title, date picker, category (10 options like academics, infrastructure), and detailed description. Stored securely in Firebase.

Dashboard & Management

View, edit, and track your past complaints in a personal dashboard. Logout anytime to end session.

Key Features

Designed for seamless user experience with fixed header/footer, impactful GUI, and smooth transitions.

  • Full validation on all forms (name characters, SRN alphanumeric 6 chars, Gmail only, phone 10 digits starting 6-9, password strength with emojis)
  • Nested dropdowns for department, level (UG/PG), and courses based on Indian education system
  • Firebase integration for user data and complaints storage/retrieval
  • Responsive design with Bootstrap, SCSS styling, and animations
  • Protected routes for logged-in users (dashboard, complaint submission)
